# Contributors

Tetrad has been under active development for more than three decades. It began in
the Philosophy Department at Carnegie Mellon University and has evolved through
the sustained work of faculty, graduate students, postdocs, software engineers,
and collaborators across several institutions. This page acknowledges the major
contributors whose work has shaped Tetrad into the platform it is today.
