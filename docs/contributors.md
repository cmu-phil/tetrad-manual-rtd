# Contributors

Tetrad has been under active development for nearly three decades. It began in the
Philosophy Department at Carnegie Mellon University and has grown through the
sustained effort of faculty, graduate students, postdocs, software engineers, and
collaborators across several institutions.

This page acknowledges the major contributors whose work has shaped Tetrad into the
platform it is today.
