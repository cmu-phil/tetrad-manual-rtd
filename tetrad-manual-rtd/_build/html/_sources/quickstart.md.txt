
# Quickstart

A five-minute tour from data to a graph:

1. **Load data** (tabular or covariance).
2. **(Optional) Add Knowledge** (tiers, required/forbidden edges).
3. **Run Search** (choose an algorithm appropriate to your data).
4. **Inspect Graph** (paths, subgraphs, edge frequencies).
5. **(Optional) Compare** to truth or alternate algorithms.
6. **(Optional) Parameterize & Simulate**.
